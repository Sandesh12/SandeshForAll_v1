-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Android application Sandesh is built for all audience who intend to help and communicate to people who deaf and dumb.The application is built considering American sign language to decode text to signs


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--Installation instructions:

Install Sandesh.apk in your android phone


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
--How to use:

1.Click on voice recognition icon to give input through voice
2.Verify text selected in the editbox.
3.Click on search
4.Other options include details of american sign language
